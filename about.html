about

Hello. My name is Ava Schmidt and I'm a rising junior at the University of Michigan studying computer science and computational cognitive science with a UX design minor.

I've always enjoyed creating things. I believe studying cs and ux design provides me with a skillset to push beyond my own abilites and create things that otherwise seem impossible. I enjoy coding as a tool to solve difficult problems and as a form of self expression through art + design.

When I'm not busy with work, I enjoy going for bike rides, playing pickleball, waking up for the sunrise, playing my guitar and cruising on my skateboard. I love keeping myself busy. Whether that be experimenting with new hobbies, volunteering, or going on random side-quests with friends.

I'm looking to graduate in spring of 2025 or earlier and plan to potentially work toward's a master's degree.

Thank you for reading and visiting my website, please reach out if you want to collaborate or meet up!
